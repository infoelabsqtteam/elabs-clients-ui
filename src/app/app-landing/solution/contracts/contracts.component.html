Contracts

Contracts - As an extension of Lab Information Management System (LIMS) application, Order Management Module provides an integration between various testing processes of a product testing from an order received stage to Invoicing stage.

Main features:-

  • Sample receiving and Order booking– Captured all the sample entries arrived at the lab for testing leaving behind no gaps.
  • Job Allocation – Allocating the orders booked to the respective section analysts.
  • Four level review process – The result of the sample goes through a four-layer review process for absolute accuracy.
  • Invoice and Dispatch – Automation of Invoice for the processed orders and updation of dispatch details.
