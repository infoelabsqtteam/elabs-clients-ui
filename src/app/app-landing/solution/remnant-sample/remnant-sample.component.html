Shelf Life Study

Shelf Life Study - While testing pharmaceuticals and food products, one of the key capability of your laboratory will be to perform a shelf life study of samples. Shelf life of the product is the time period for which the product maintains its identity and quality when stored at the conditions defined on the label of the product. LIMS allows you to effectively maintain samples and periodically book the testing as per the defined protocol of the laboratory.
