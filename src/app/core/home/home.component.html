Insolvency Management

Interim Resolution Process

The enormity of tasks for the interim resolution require very meticulous planning and a trained workforce which can do multi-tasking rapidly. MarketClear offers you one of its kind detailed technology platform that can help you manage the interim resolution process very easily while keeping your data with industry leading security. You can generate all different types of regulatory forms and accept data from creditors electronically. With a conferencing solution and and an integrated e-voting system the vote capture and management of creditors can all be done with ease. In-built task management feature helps you keep track of all the tasks with their urgency level all the time.

Process Management

Several management and control tasks require presence of a very senior and seasoned process manager to help the RP control and administer activities with the debtor. MarketClear has partnered with a number of highly experienced professionals who can help manage the whole resolution process and thereby helping the RP with right decision making during the CIRP. Nature of business at the corporate debtor sometimes require professionals who not only have ability but also relevant experience of the industry. MarketClear simplifies such engagement by vetting, hiring of such experienced individuals and training them on the required CIRP protocol. This facility is available on-demand.

Data Room and Retention

CIRP may generate a huge amount of data which may be extremely sensitive and therefore need to be stored and managed securely by the IRP/RP. MarketClear offers a state of art data storage and retention service with proper authentication and authorization ensuring no unauthorized access to data by anyone. The data protection policy can be defined by the IRP/RP and they can control the access as per their requirements. Post the completion of the process, all data can be retained for 7 years OR more as required in safe system allowing on-demand access to authorized personnel.

Secretarial Support Service

MarketClear understands the complexity of the tasks that an IRP OR an RP has to handle on a daily basis. Due to the extremely high volume of cases, it is not a surprise that an IP/RP might be handling multiple cases at a time leading to extremely high work force requirement. With MarketClear on-demand secretarial support service, all your support tasks are taken care of by our highly trained workforce specially trained to handle proceedings as defined by the rules under The Code.
